about

how much of the original vision can survive the process of building it?

that question matters to me because building something always changes it. the work is keeping enough of the core idea alive while dealing with tools, limits, taste, performance, and all the decisions in between.

i am oğuzhan. i work on games, tools, and low-level software. this site is where i try to express that work on my own terms: process, direction, experiments, and whatever helps the idea make it into the world.
