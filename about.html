about

how much of the original vision can survive the process of building it?

i work on games, tools, and low-level software. before software, i studied music, made films, and later studied at 42. that mix still affects how i look at projects: timing, image, discipline, systems.

i like old-school software practice: direct tools, understanding what is happening underneath, and respecting the job. i also care about art direction, mood, precision, and how a project feels. for me, games are where those concerns meet.

i have a lot of respect for people who are deeply into their work: people who push the craft, share what they make, and let their love for the job show through the details. this site is where i keep my own process visible: devlogs, experiments, tools, notes, and small finished things as they happen.
